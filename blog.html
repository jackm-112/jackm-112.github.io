01: cool songs

now that the site's been fully re-written, i thought it'd be fun to share some cool songs i've been listening to lately. keep in mind that i'm not a music reviewer by any means so don't be expecting in-depth write-ups.

Chubby Pitcher56 - Pitcher56 1st Album
after not putting out any new music in over a decade, Pitcher56 came back in 2022 with the (aptly..?) titled 'Pitcher56 1st Album' and this opening track is so good. the piano is my favourite instrument and it's difficult for me not to love songs that include it.

If You Want It Niteflyte - Niteflyte
the guitar in the opening to this one seriously reminded me of 'Sparkle' by Tatsuro Yamashita, one of my all-time favourite songs + artists. if you can find an upload somewhere, i implore you to take a listen. as for 'If You Want It,' the strings are really nice and the vocals are just amazing. plus, the hook "if you want it, you can get it, with your love" is super catchy.

Fine Whine (feat. Joe Fox, Future & M.I.A.) A$AP Rocky - AT.LONG.LAST.A$AP
yet again, im a sucker for piano. i adore Rocky's experimental production, especially on his 2018 album 'TESTING.' i must've listened to this song on repeat a dozen times yesterday while drawing.

Bubbles jenny01 - jenny01 Best
this song shares a lot of vibes with 'Chubby.' 1:40 is incredible, especially with headphones on. very cheerful-sounding song all around. this is the type of music i'd like to make someday. :)
