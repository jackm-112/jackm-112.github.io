bio

cleft is a young white bee with a love for music and an infectious charisma. he's proficient with countless instruments: chances are that if you toss one his way, he'll be able to play you something on it!

his best friend in the whole wide world is a wasp named pitch. how did they meet again? that's a story for another time... anyways, the two get along great. they also live together!

cleft's an energetic little guy, full of positivity! there's not a negative thought in his head. (not like he's got much free space up there anyways.) he's always willing to help, pitching in however he can!

(fun fact: if you count his arms and antennae, it's like he's got five stripes: the same as a musical staff!)

species....bee
gender.....male
height.....tiny
weight.....lightweight
likes......music (the piano's his favourite!!)
