bio

puffy is a dandelion-like being that just kinda showed up one day. honestly, they're not even sure where they came from themselves. the first bug to make contact puffy was cleft, who found them wandering around after a rainstorm. during this encounter, cleft gave them the name puffy. puffy's leaf cloak was handmade by lena and it's their most treasured possession.

lacking a mouth, puffy is mute. even so, they're very expressive and easy to read. while puffy is easily entertained, they're also easily spooked: they only feel comfortable with cleft or lena and tend to disappear around others.

puffy enjoys catching a breeze and floating along. it's their preferred method of travel. and unlike an actual dandelion, their puffball head never disperses, no matter how strong the winds get.
